Our History

Welcome to the journey of Mr.Healthy Pharmacy, where our commitment to health and community began founded in 2024 by Mr.Healthy, a passionate pharmacist with a vision for superior healthcare services, Mr.Healthy Pharmacy has grown from a humble neighborhood pharmacy to a trusted healthcare provider in Yangon.

Our Vision

At Mr.Healthy Pharmacy, our vision is to be the leading provider of healthcare solutions, known for our unwavering commitment to excellence, integrity, and compassionate care. We strive to empower individuals and families in Yangon and beyond to live healthier, happier lives through accessible, high-quality healthcare products and services.

Our Mission

At Mr.Healthy Pharmacy, our mission is simple: to provide reliable access to medications and healthcare products that enhance the quality of life for our customers. We believe in building lasting relationships based on trust, respect, and superior service. Whether you're visiting us for a prescription refill, seeking advice on managing a health condition, or exploring our range of wellness products, you can count on us to prioritize your health and deliver personalized care that exceeds your expectations.
